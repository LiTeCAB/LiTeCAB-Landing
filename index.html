Product walkthrough

A quick path from startup screen to export-ready analysis.

  1. 1

    Start or load a project

    Open LiTeCAB and choose New Project for templates/custom uploads or Load Saved for existing work.

  2. 2

    Select and shape your process model

    Pick a manufacturing/recycling template, then edit inputs, units, and costs based on your scenario assumptions.

  3. 3

    Run LCA and TEA

    Generate TRACI impact and cost outputs, inspect process-level contributions, and validate what drives your totals.

  4. 4

    Export and share

    Export results for reporting, stakeholder review, and side-by-side scenario comparisons.
